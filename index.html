What is Snipp Hub?

Snipp Hub is a website similar to Snipp Hubbin where you can store any piece of code, and generate links for easy sharing

However, what makes Snipp Hub special is that it works with no database, and no back-end code.
Instead, the data is compressed and stored entirely in the link that you share, nowhere else!

Because of this design:

🗑️ Your data cannot be deleted from Snipp Hub
🔞 Your data cannot be censored
👁️ The server hosting Snipp Hub (or any clone of it) cannot read or access your data
⏳ Your data will be accessible forever (as long as you have the link)
🔍 Google will not index your data, even if your link is public
Snipp Hub cannot decompress the URL

It's possible that you clicked on an invalid link
